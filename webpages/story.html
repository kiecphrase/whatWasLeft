What Was Left, and What Came From It

On the Basis of Becoming Gods




There Was a time before, and it didn't matter.

In the beginning, we were nothing. There were many like us, and we did not stand on our own. Ours was simply existence, and we attempted to thrive.

We met a wizard who asked us a favor. Could we please help protect them? What we did was what we thought was right.

A village was trapped and needed someone to assist. There were many paths we could have chosen, but in the end we picked just one.

We found a tired farmer, alone in his field. He seemed content with the sunrise, but beckoned us over to have a chat.
